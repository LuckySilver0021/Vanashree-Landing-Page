# 1. Organization Overview

**Name:** Vanashree Gramvikas Pratishthan  
**Location:** Gatewadi, Taluka Parner, District Ahilyanagar, Maharashtra, India  
**Type:** Non-Profit Organization (NGO)  
**Focus Areas:** Environmental Conservation & Rural Development  

## About Us

Vanashree Gramvikas Pratishthan is a grassroots organization working towards sustainable rural development through environmental conservation and community participation.

Our work focuses on increasing green cover, supporting rural education infrastructure, and encouraging people—especially youth—to take responsibility for nature and their villages.

We believe that true development begins with strong roots: **nature, education, and community unity.**

---

# 2. Our Mission

- Promote large-scale tree plantation and ecological balance.
- Increase awareness about environmental sustainability.
- Strengthen rural school infrastructure.
- Build long-term, self-sustaining village ecosystems.

---

# 3. Our Work

## 🌳 Tree Plantation & Environmental Activities

- **600+ trees planted and distributed** in Gatewadi and surrounding areas.
- Plantation of **native, shade-giving, and fruit-bearing species**.
- Focus on **biodiversity improvement and long-term ecological impact**.
- Active involvement of **local community, youth, and children**.
- Ongoing **care and monitoring** of planted trees.

---

## 🏫 Educational & Infrastructure Support

In **Gatewadi, Taluka Parner, District Ahilyanagar**:

- Construction of a **water tank for a local school**, ensuring access to water.
- **Donation of land** for school development, supporting long-term educational growth.
- Support initiatives aimed at **improving rural educational facilities**.
